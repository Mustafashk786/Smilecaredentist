Dental Implants

Dental implants are a permanent, modern solution for missing teeth. They are designed to look and feel like natural teeth and are the gold standard for restoring your smile and oral health.

How Dental Implants Work

A dental implant consists of a titanium post that is surgically placed into the jawbone, where it acts as a new tooth root. A custom-made crown is then attached to this post, providing a stable and natural-looking replacement tooth. [attachment_0](attachment) This process not only restores your smile but also prevents jawbone deterioration.

Benefits of Dental Implants

  • **Permanent Solution:** Unlike dentures, implants are a long-lasting, durable solution.
  • **Preserves Jawbone:** They stimulate the jawbone, preventing bone loss that often occurs after tooth loss.
  • **Natural Appearance:** Implants look, feel, and function just like your natural teeth.
  • **Improved Speech:** Missing teeth can affect speech, but implants restore your ability to speak clearly.
