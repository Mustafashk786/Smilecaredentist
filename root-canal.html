Root Canal Treatment

A root canal is a common and highly effective procedure to save a tooth that has been severely damaged by decay or infection. Modern techniques make the process relatively painless, and it is a better alternative to tooth extraction.

The Procedure

During the treatment, the infected pulp and nerve are carefully removed from the tooth's root. The inside of the tooth is then cleaned, disinfected, and sealed to prevent any further infection. Finally, a crown is placed to restore the tooth's strength and function.

Benefits

  • Relieves severe tooth pain
  • Saves the natural tooth from extraction
  • Allows you to maintain your natural smile
