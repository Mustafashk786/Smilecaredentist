General Dentistry

General dentistry is the foundation of our practice. We focus on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of common dental diseases and conditions. Regular checkups are key to maintaining a healthy smile for a lifetime.

Our General Dentistry Services Include:

  • **Routine Checkups & Cleanings:** Regular visits to prevent future problems and maintain good oral hygiene.
  • **Tooth Fillings:** We use tooth-colored fillings to repair cavities and restore the tooth's function.
  • **Extractions:** Safe and painless tooth removal when a tooth is beyond saving.
  • **Dental Crowns and Bridges:** Custom-made restorations to cover damaged teeth or replace missing ones.
