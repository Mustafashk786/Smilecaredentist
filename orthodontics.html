Orthodontics

Orthodontics is the branch of dentistry that focuses on correcting misaligned teeth and jaws. A perfectly aligned smile not only looks great but also improves your overall oral health and function.

Our Orthodontic Solutions

We offer a range of solutions to straighten teeth and correct bite issues, tailored to meet the needs of both children and adults. Our treatments include:

  • **Metal Braces:** The traditional and most effective method for complex cases.
  • **Ceramic Braces:** A more discreet option than metal braces, using tooth-colored brackets.
  • **Clear Aligners:** A popular, nearly invisible way to straighten teeth using a series of custom-made trays. [attachment_1](attachment) They are removable and offer great flexibility.
